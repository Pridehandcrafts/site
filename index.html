  • About

    Pride Handcrafts

    At Pride Handcrafts, we take pride in curating a diverse and vibrant collection that spans accross traditional and contemporary art styles, catering to individuals of all ages. Our artisans are dedicated to exceeding the expectations of our clients, and we achieve this by meticulously crafting a wide range of macrame bracelets. These unnique pieces are skillfully made from scratch, utilizing premium twine and on eclectic selection of beads, ensuring each creation is a testament to both artistry and quality.

    In addition to our commitment to jewellery, Pride Handcrafts is passionate about encapsulating memories in the form of parsonalized gifts. We take pre-made charms, intricately designed beaded bags, and stylish keyholders to craft unique token that mark the beginning of cherished new memories.

  • CEO

    Thandiwe Mhlambe

    Passionate and innovative, I'm a self-taught jewellery artison with two years of experience. I specialize in hand-assembled pieces crafted for both private clients and NGOs. My focus is on creating timeless and personalized jewellery that people can proudly wear and cherish. I find joy in the artistry of the process, ensuring each piece reflects the individual style and preferences of the wearer. I've also had the privilege of collaborating with NGOs to create meaningful pieces with a purpose. My commitment is to design jewelery that goes beyond trends, becoming enduring symbols of pride and parsonal stories.
